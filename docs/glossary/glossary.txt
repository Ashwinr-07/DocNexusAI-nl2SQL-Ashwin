ICD-10: International Classification of Diseases, 10th Revision — standard diagnostic codes
CPT: Current Procedural Terminology — used to describe procedures and services
HCP: Healthcare Professional — includes doctors, specialists, NPs
HCO: Healthcare Organization — hospitals, health systems, clinics, ASCs
Claim: A record of a billed interaction between a patient and provider
Patient: The individual receiving treatment or services
Procedure: A medical operation or clinical service performed
Drug: A prescribed or administered pharmaceutical product
Therapy Area: The medical area or specialty the product/procedure targets
KOL: Key Opinion Leader — highly influential HCPs
Trial: A registered clinical study used to evaluate drugs/devices
